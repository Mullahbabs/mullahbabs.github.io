About Us

Seventy7Soccer Ltd., a Calabar-based sports development company, aspires to revolutionize sports standards and cultivate high-quality talent in Nigeria. We provide a world-class curriculum that equips young athletes with the technical and tactical skills needed to compete internationally. Our founder, Fidel Egbe, a passionate coach and mentor, returns to his home state, Cross River, to share his expertise and build the next generation of sports stars.

What we Offer

international Standards

At Seventy7Soccer, we are committed to upholding the highest international standards in sports development. Our training programs and operational procedures are benchmarked against the best practices in the global sporting world, ensuring that our athletes are equipped with the skills and discipline to compete at the highest levels.

World Class Coaches

Our academy boasts a team of world-class coaches who bring unparalleled expertise and experience to the table. With a passion for developing talent, our coaches are dedicated to nurturing young athletes into professional stars, using cutting-edge techniques and personalized coaching strategies.

State of the art facilities

Seventy7Soccer offers access to state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to provide athletes with the ideal environment to grow and excel. Our facilities include modern training grounds, advanced equipment, and recovery centers, all tailored to support the physical and mental well-being of our athletes.

International Agencies

We collaborate with top international agencies to provide our athletes with exposure to global opportunities. Our partnerships ensure that athletes are connected with talent scouts, clubs, and organizations worldwide, facilitating seamless transitions into professional careers both locally and internationally.

Why Us?

Mr Egbe's experience as a UEFA licensed soccer coach, currently leading the Orchard Valley Toros FC. His dedication to developing young talent is evident in his coaching career.
